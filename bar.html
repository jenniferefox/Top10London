Bars in Fitzrovia

Going out with friends, with colleagues after work, or on a hot date? Look no further! Our top 5 choices in Fitrovia are below

To the bar!

No.1 Aquaspirit, Oxford Circus

Rooftop bar, perfect for Friday night after-work drinks.

No. 2 Vagabond, Charlotte Street

Your go-to for a chilled glass of wine with colleagues after work.

No. 3 Mr. Fogg's House of Botanicals

Want to impress? Head to Mr. Foggs for a secret garden escape with killer cocktails.
