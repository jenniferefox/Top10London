London Grind is a buzzing riverside espresso bar, cocktail bar and restaurant.

London Grind is a discerning brunch and dinner spot for south of the river, and a favourite dinner destination for the locals.

Bookings & Reservations 
We keep most of our tables free for walk-ins. At busy times you might have to wait, but we’ll do our best to find you a table as quickly as possible. If there is space, you’ll be able to grab a drink at the bar while you wait. Please note we only seat parties when everyone has arrived.  For parties larger than 12 please email us to book your table.

Weekend Brunch - Saturdays and Sundays, 11am-5pm 
For weekend brunch, we keep most of our tables free for walk ins for groups of up to six. We only take reservations for groups of six. For weekend brunch bookings, your table is yours for up to 90 minutes.

At All Other Times
We take reservations for groups of any size at all other times. For parties larger than 12 please email us to book your table. London Grind is also available for exclusive private hire. 

Running late? 
Please note we can only hold your table for 10 minutes after your reservation time. Should you arrive later, we'll do our best to seat you, but your booking will be treated as a walk-in.

Children 
Children are welcome in London Grind until 6pm. Due to space constraints, prams are not permitted inside the restaurant, but can be left in the lobby area at the owners risk. Unfortunately we do not have a children's menu, or baby changing facilities.
